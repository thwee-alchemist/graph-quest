Rules

Welcome to Graph Quest. Every player starts with a home star producing 10 ships per round. A round ends when all players have clicked "ready".
Repeat this process for each star you want to dispatch ships from. Finally, click ready, to let the server know you are done with your round.

Privacy

Any data you enter here, including name, ready state, and moves are recorded and logged on the server. That said, I don't really read that stuff unless there's a problem.
